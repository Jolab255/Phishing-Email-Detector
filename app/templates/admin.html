Administrator Instructions

To allow new users to link their Gmail accounts, you must manually add them as "Test Users" in the Google Cloud Console. Otherwise, Google will block their access with a "Verification required" error.

  1. Go to the OAuth Consent Screen in your Google Cloud Project.
  2. Scroll down to the "Test users" section.
  3. Click "+ ADD USERS" and enter the user's Gmail address.
  4. Once added in Google Console, click "Verify" in the list below to grant them access to this app.
